Traveling around the world by Petr Josifek





1. part - Trip into Romania

This work could be a warning for you, this story could teach you how to travel or can be just fun for you. My story begins on Sunday 31.8.1997, perhaps in a moment, when Lady Diana dies.

1.9.1997

I met my friend Jacob. We answered a couple of important emails in our University. I did successfully complete registration for further semester. We bought some food, because we did not want to have empty backpacks. We left by city bus the second largest city of Czech Republic. This city is Brno. We moved southwards on motorway. Direction was already identified. It was south. We planned to move to Slovakia, city of Bratislava. We started walking but after about 20 minutes when we reached a petrol station, we found out, then we had made a mistake, because we were on a worse road. At this petrol station we learned that it will be very hard for us use this way to get to city Bratislava. Maybe it could be possible, if we went through the city of Olomouc, but this city is on a totally different way for us at this moment. I never thought that our beautiful and native country had another highway than from city Plzen through Prague to city Bratislava. As we learned, that we had used our energy for nothing. We had to turn back to Brno. We walked back again for about 20 minutes to Brno. We turned left. After 100 meters we reached another petrol station and my friend asked a lift to south. The driver took us to city Bratislava. He was speaking about trouble in this part of Czech Republic. We got to Slovakia and after we reached the capital of Slovakia, Bratislava. We visited Slovak's Bronx and other historical parts of this metropolis our Slovak brothers. Czech and Slovak Republic split to two Republics on the 1.1.1993, it was without any war how some people thought, we are not Yugoslavia, but former Czechoslovakia. We still call Slovakian people our brothers. We were offered a lift. We called this driver by nickname Adventurer, because he helped us as much as he could. He dropped us without reason on Slovak border with Hungary. He told us, that he would have liked to continue with us south, but he had no passport in that moment. So we got the border to Hungary. We passed the border and we asked a girl to take us to Budapest. But she had paperwork at border. So we have to wait for her. So we were sitting and we were looking into the map and we are trying to get a lift into Budapest. Nobody took us. The girl finished the paperwork and she asked us to get into the car. After few hours she continued the other way. So we left her car on petrol station. It was our first contact with a driver who did not speak in Slav language, so we have to start using at this moment communication in Germany or in English. Slav languages are very similar. I can understand Slovak, Polish, Ukraine, Russian, Slovenian and many more languages, because they are similar with Czech. We reached somewhere near a town Tata Bana. We began to feel the value of a car for us. We asked a young couple, who took us to the center of Hungarian capital. We have been in Budapest. We found the pole star and got where is north to have idea, where we are, and where we are going. We crossed cuddliest bridge and fell asleep in sleeping bags over one side of the river Duane.

2.9.1997

It was very hard for us to get, what part of city we had been in. It was maybe because we have never been before in such a large city like Budapest. By bus we pull out through centers, but how we got to the petrol station in south part of city Budapest, we found, that we are still relatively very near center. So it was very hard to find somebody who was going out of city. So we used bus one more time and we tried to find some nice place for next hitchhiking to city Szeged. We got a lift from some young man. He has fridge on the car. After all he dropped us on road to Szeged. After that one more gentleman took us and he helped us to get better directions to south, it was very important 5 kilometres, because we had already been on the highway to south. One cowboy stopped on motorway and he said that he was going into city Subotice, but we told him, that we are going to city Szeget. After he left, we found town Subotice on map. Subotice was our destination after city Szeged, because it was in former Yugoslavia, in Serbia. If we took that lift, we could be today already in the capitol of Serbia, in city Beograd, because that driver was driving very very fast. After all we stopped a truck of a very old date of registration. Smiling man from Serbia sat inside. It was long, but very funny way. He spoke in his Slav language and we could understand what he is saying about his life. It was man full of experience. He was crossing often the whole of Europe. Also he spoke about mystique of southern countries. Fifty kilometres per hour we drove to the south. When we got to the border with Serbia, he told us to pack up and wait for him on second part of border. He does not want to have people in car in this moment. We reached the Hungarian custom office and we were getting stamps, we reached into the Serbian customs office and we were getting kicked out, because we did not have tourist visa to Serbia. Yep, yep this was out of mind of our new friend from Serbia. He had told us, that we could visit Serbia, but he had not told us we needed a visa. Also the travel agency told us before we started this trip, that we could visit any country in Europe without tourist visa, but they for sure forgot about Serbia. Here we got angry in most possible level. We walked back. One lady took as few kilometres away from border zone. We opened the camp in woods after that. We found out, that we had left road atlas in the car of Serbian man. We also found that we could get into Greece over Romania and Bulgaria. We opened the Indian fire to make some food. We slept well.

3.9.1997

We woke up in late morning hours. We left the woods and we got back on the road. We had no idea, how far we had to comeback by road to north to get some road to Romania. Maybe we have to comeback the long way to the north, who knows. We got a lift to some main road eastwards. Our mood was very low. One driver took us on junction to Romania. Friend of mine, Jacob wanted to call home, but unsuccessful, because automatic machine demanded for calling the minimum charge and he want to call collect without any Hungarian coins, because we did not have any of Hungarian coins. We took fresh water. We made notice "Romania" on paper to show others, where we wanted to go. Two girls were about 50 meters from us. They were hitchhiking to Romania too, but they had no reason to speak with us, because they got a lift in few minutes. After a while we saw a car, which was driving from Romania. Driver asked us, where is Hungary. In this moment he looked very funny, because he did not know where he was. He turned his car and asked us to sit. We had no idea, what was going on. My friend Jacob said to me, that it is probably the driver, who drove here few minutes ago and he comeback for us now, maybe he returned for the couple of girls in first reason, who knows. We were coming into Romania. We crossed border to Romania, but it did not looks like the border, it was like a big market. About 100 local men were bidding world currencies to make some profits by cheating here. We got information in customs exchange office that one Germany mark is little bit more that 4000 Romanian Lei. Really when we crossed border, it was totally different world. Everybody asked to do business with us. We felt safety because we were very near border, so a lot of policemen were around us. After this experience we stopped a local Romanian horseman with car, but we found in minutes, than it is taxi driver and he wanted money. We told him, that if he is taxi driver then he needs to have taxi schedule on his car. We did not find out even tariffs or something like this. He answered, that he is not official taxi driver, so it is cheaper for us. But we really did not want to use taxi to Athens. If we planed this trip in this way, we can use bus or plane. But we were poor students and we chose this way because it was more fun and full of experience than just boring and sitting in bus or in plane. We got other car. They looked very Christian, but they asked us for money too. We had no idea, why everybody wants money here in Romania. My friend got angry again. We started to speak about going back to civilization, it is mean to Hungary. We were planning to go back and visit Italy or other country in that moment. We discussed about this problem a little bit. I wanted to continue, but my friend Jacob wanted to go back. We were thinking of taking the train to Sofia, capitol of Bulgaria or at least into Bucurest, but it was expensive for us too. My friend got an idea, then we have to ask first, if they want any money to drop us somewhere. So we could continue to south. On the way into town Arad I found a full box of cigarettes, I told this to my friend that we could use this box of cigarettes to stop some smoker driver, but my friend Jacob wanted to smoke it. We got into town Arad and we reached the petrol station. There we found successfully another adventurer of this trip. He wanted to take us at least into further city. He took it like fun, because he had not other reason to drive into next city. And when we reached other city then he showed us history part of this city. It was city Timisvar.

After that he left us alone. We found an impressive church and we sat in park, which people knew like revolutionary place of flight with communist dictator of Romania, Mr. Caucesko. We looked at some girl, who danced in the open air. Second day we found out, she should be crazy. We made a lot of new friends, who started to speak to us about the world. We spoke about our trip to Greece. We got information, that if we wanted to go to Greece, than we had to go first into Bucurest, it is capitol of Romania. We have to cross bridge trough river Duane and it is possible only over there. We spoke with a man, who wanted to work in Germany, but he could not cross border to get there, because nobody wanted to give him a visa for entry to Germany. We listened to some local music. My friend started to smoke with the others. We discuss friendly with local policemen and fell asleep.

4.9.1997

In the morning we woke up, but already without pack of cigarettes, which perhaps the children from previous day took. We spend many hours to get out from city Timishar. This city was without signs of name of streets, even of signs to show us, where the motorway was to Bucurest. After that we got to the motorway and we got good luck from a Romanian driver from city Oradea he invited us for a burger and he took us almost into Bucurest. After that we took a truck on the highway into the Bucurest. When we tried to ask about the historical center and any information about Bucurest we met two local university students. We bought some alcohol together and we started a party in College. One of the students was from city Constants, which is on the beach of Black sea. So we changed plans and we thought, then it could be a good idea to visit the Black sea, because we didn’t have enough time for trip to Greece yet. We fell asleep after that.

5.9.1997

This morning we were awaking and saying bye to our friends. We spent some time in the Bucurest, because we liked to check this capital of Romania. We were using local bus to get out of the city Bucurest. In local bus we met group of about five boys and they wanted money from us, because we did not have a ticket. They were very nice to us, because we were not citizen of Romania. They asked us for about 10 USD for penalty or we can visit police station. But we did not want to spend time in police station so we gave them about 5 USD in German currency and it was enough for them, because we were in Romania and there is was very fix prices at this time. We left city Bucurest and after a few hours we reached a tourist resort on the beach of Black sea. I called home to say hello from the Black sea. We slept on the beach with night view on the sea about 60 kilometres south from seaport of city Constants.

6.9.1997

This day was full of the rest. We did swimming and playing games with sands on the beach, no more major activities in this day. In the evening we started the return journey. Because we had for this trip only 2 weeks, it was just because my friend did not register himself for next semester on University yet and he had to do it before the 15.9.1997. We started hitchhiking about 60 kilometres to north back into seaport city Constants. We were looking for the way to Bucurest and also to the town Tosca, because we wanted to visit delta of river Duane and town Galati.

7.9.1997

This day did not start optimistically. I can say that every day started in pessimism on this trip, in negative mood, but every evening we were full of new experiences. We could not move out in this morning from city Constants. Till afternoon we were waiting on the road, but no car took us to Bucurest. We met one Romanian girl with CD-player. We got friendly and with her we successfully reached in late hours in three cars into Bucurest. Girl was living in Bucurest, so she showed us a local park, where we could spend this night. People played chess in this night for hours in this park. So I joined some games of chess. After that I went to sleep.

8.9.1997

In morning we found out, that somebody needed our old shoes. I had others in my backpack, but my friend was at this moment without shoes. In same moment the policeman came. We met him in the evening hours. He had found some shoes on a trailer not so far from us in market place. It was a good pair of shoes for my friend. His father later said to him, that he got better shoes there was before. We tried to use internet in Bucurest, but nobody would let us to do so. There was not internet coffee. So we continued back to north. We crossed city Brasow and we visited castle of legend vampire named Dracula. Below the castle was a small market place, which made business from legends of Transylvania. We continued to other tourist town Faragas, which one boy from Black sea beach told us to visit. We met two local girls, who were very nice to us. They spent some hours with us and after they tried to find some rooms for us in the house of some local people, but without success. They moved with us to the train station, where we could spend a safe night.

9.9.1997

We continued the next day to city Glus. From there we called home. I lost my watch there. Probably I left it by the phone. We took a truck to the border to Hungary. We were delayed on the way, because there was some problem on the road. And our driver had not slept for last 3 days. So he slept, when he could. It was fun to look at him and see that he was able to sleep and drive in some seconds. At two o'clock at night of Hungarian time and at three o'clock at night of Romanian time we cross the border to Hungary. We had been in Hungary. So we felt safe enough to say good night to each other.

10.9.1997

This day was in a mood of great depression. We couldn’t visit Venice in Italy, because we did not have enough time for it yet. I woke up in a field. I can say for sure on very special place. This place, where I woke up was exactly a place, where nobody can see you from the side of the road. So I found out at that morning, then I slept on very special place. It was a dirty place as people had used it for toilet purposes, I am sorry but it was exactly, how I just wrote. Place where we went hitchhiking was prohibited for 30 kilometres. So we walked the whole day. We visited restaurant. We got some soup and rest. After that we continued to walk. In the evening we made a fire and we had some supper. It was good food, so we were full of energy for next day. We reached the zone, where we could hitchhike again, so we believed, we could reach Budapest next day. By the way, this time we used other way, as when we was hitchhiking from Budapest to south, this time we want to rich Budapest from east.

11.9.1997

Next day we started hitchhiking to Budapest. First we had to wait for a car for a long time. Some man gave us some cookies. After that somebody from the village took us out from this crazy place. After that everything became very fast. In seconds we got another car and in a few hours we reached Budapest. After that we continued by highway to Vienna, capital of Austria. We made fire again. We fell asleep not far from the highway.

12.9.1997

In the morning we got very fast to Viennese airport. We washed our bodies a little bit and had some fun to get in better mood. We moved by hitchhiking to center of Vienna. It was nice to be in a clean city. We had been for long time on the highway and in Romania. We got into park of Sigmund Freud. There is we were invited by a group of our generation for the next day to the music party. We found in park the monument, which was full of old motherboards of ours lovely computers. It was funny. There we fell asleep. It was my first night in former capitalistic block.

13.9.1997

This day we wanted to stay in Vienna. I had been in Vienna in summer 1990 yet. So I visited Mariahilfestrasse again. It is a famous street in Vienna. That street is full of the shops. It was famous shopping street for Vienna in that time, similar to Wall Street in New York for trade market. My friend was still sleeping. I bought some CDs, videotapes, and film for my camera, because it was still cheaper there is then in Czech in that days. After we took local train together with our music group to the east part of Vienna near small lake, where we swam in the rain, smoked grass and played music. Our new friends had no spare clothing to dress, so they used our sweaters. After that we made a date for next day in park in center of Vienna and we slept in our tent, because it was raining.

14.9.1997

My friend has to visit our faculty for registration at next day. So we have to move from Vienna fast. But we have to wait for our friends from previous day, because they still have Jacob's sweater. After all we left address for them there is with somebody who knows them. We left Vienna, because it was already afternoon. On twice we got Czech Republic. From city of Mikulov we got another car to Velke Mezirici, it was city of my high school. I was planning to visit here some my old friends. My friend Jacob had for few minutes some problem with stomach, because he ate plums from tree on the way. I did same, but I did not have any problems. It was maybe, because I am eating many of fruits since I was very small. After he called at home and his father took us home, because it was no so far.

Until the 4 of February 1998 I have been registered like student of University.



2. part - Trip to Israel, Egypt and ...

8.2.1998

My friend Wenceslas flew tree weeks before me so I quickly followed him. There should be work in Israel. I bought air ticket, and I paid mediation and I flew presto to Geneva and after into Tel-Aviv. In plane I met two boys who were going with me to the same work agency. In Tel-Aviv we found the local agency. The Girls from this office contacted some farmer in Galilea, north part of Israel. We took the bus to city Afula. It is near Nazareth. After we called the farmer and he took us to his village. There we had to take care of his vineyard. Many times I visited Palestine villages, because this place was on border with Palestine. Palestine people are always hospitable. It is different between West and East. West people are so much more civilized. East prefers so much family. After a month we got three weeks of holidays, because vine need to grow up. My two friends from plane were going to visit Israel. And I came to visit my friend Wenceslaw to south of Israel. It is a very small country. I got a car to Nazareth Lake, so famous lake from the Bible, from New Testament. I reached Sea in a few hours too. Second day in evening I reached place of my friend Wenceslaw. I got some information about Egypt. I spent two nights there. This place is on the border with Jordan, it is the biggest moshav in Israel named En-Yahav. I hitchhiked to Red Sea. It is southern point of Israel. I visited Bedouin in his tent. Next day I crossed border to Egypt and I reached monument of St. Kathleen from 4. century below Mousses hill in Sinai. I met a group of workers in this place and they helped me to move to Cairo. I slept in a house of one of them that night.

21.3.1998

Next day my new friend, from previous day, helped me to visit the Pyramids and the Sphinx in Gaza (Cairo). He was catholic. I used the train to Aswan (1000 kilometres to south). I almost got Sudan, but ship to Sudan is going once in week only, so I had not time for it. I visited great Aswan dams and some sights. I met Czech group, what I met few weeks ago in Czech Republic, they are friends of my friend. They were planning this trip for years. But I did not have any idea a few weeks ago about this trip to Egypt, even to visit Israel. I was sleeping in cheap hotels. It was funny to be in this country. I could swim on the beach. I can check culture and religion. I could play games. I could drink beer or whatever I like to do here. I visited Kom-ombo, Luxury, Hurgada (Red sea), Suez,... I continued back to Sinai. I reached Israel by cheap taxi. It was not so far from Palestine zone of Gaza. I got a car to Dead Sea. Next day I visited Jerusalem. There I found a group of young people from Czech Republic and they showed me new information about work and living style in this country. I continued thought oldest city of world, it is Jericho, back to my farmer and my friends from farm. I stayed two more months over there.

Tel-Aviv

1.6.1998

I left the farm. I moved to city Tel-Aviv, largest city of Israel. I slept in a cheap hotel with other Czechs. After I met some other Czech group and I had been with them in Czech flat for 100 USD per month. I could work. It was no problem. I can work twice in week to have some money to spend. I worked in restaurant, like remover, painter,... But I did not work so much here, because I like to have some fun on the beach in the night. I wanted to visit Jordan or Egypt with one local girl, her name is Ilanit, but she cancelled this trip. So I left Israel. When I left the beach, the water had 32 degree of Celsius.

17.7.1998

I took plain into Zurich, Switzerland. I did not use my return ticket from Zurich to Prague, because I would like to visit Amsterdam and Paris. In Switzerland I met girl, who want to visit Amsterdam next day. So I spend night in her house. Next day we did stopover and we visited musical in Germany. After Amsterdam I visited Paris for few days. I checked Louver, Versailles and Eiffel tower. Paris was full of people from Slovakia, Czech, Poland and Russia. After that I returned by hitchhiking to Amsterdam, because I left my backpack in flat of one Polish girl. Next day I used hitchhiking to city Dresden. It is few kilometres from border of Czech Republic. After that I took train to my hometown. I have been for 6 months with my family and friends in Czech Republic.

11.1.1999

I took plane to New York. I bought a car in Virginia for 600 USD after a few months. I drove to Chicago, after Cleveland, Buffalo and visited Niagara falls, after Boston, World Trade Center in New York, Washington D.C., Atlanta,...

12.12.1999

After 11 months I came back to Europe. I met some girls from Latin America in plane. I met also my friend Vlad. We celebrate together Millennium in Paris. In few weeks I was able to speak in my native language again and I made new trips such to Italy, London, Scotland and Belfast.



3. part - Trip around the world for two and half years

15.10.2000

I spoke about this trip with Vlad for about 2 years. Date was fixed at 15. 10. 2000. Vlad visited embassy of Syria. They gave tourist visa of Syria for us. We spent last weekend in Czech Republic with group of about 5 ours friends on trip in south east of Czech Republic in country of wine. There is we met with Jana, who we met also in Egypt and Japan after. I got the train with Vlad. We got on the highway to Bratislava. We use same way to Hungary, like I did 3 years ago with Jacob. We pass Slovakia and in evening we went around Budapest, capital of Hungary. Second day we slept in middle of Romania. Next day we have been on border with Bulgaria. And next day we have been already in Turkey. We came into orient, Turkey. It was amazing. Mosques were everywhere. We met local police and fall asleep. Next day we started hitchhiking into Istanbul. Vlad was already here once. So we got hostel and we used internet to check emails. We did meditation in some mosques. We visited local bazaar. We got to understand, that Dagmar Havel was here few days ago. She was wife of president of Czech Republic in these days. After 3 days we left this almost largest city of the world and we got into Asia. Still we were hitchhiking. One of the drivers took us to original Turkish bath. It was very cold weather in these days in Europe yet. We found new summer season in south of Turkey. We entered to Syria.

About 23.10.2000

We entered into Syrian historical city Aleppo. This country looks very poor. It is interesting to have some country so close to Europe, only few hours buy car, so different like this. Totality regime has power here. People are not free and it is make them so poor. I played with local people chess and I smoked some local things. I met one local chess player. He spoke very good English. He could live in England, because his father is from UK, but he liked to study here, because it is cheaper and he had a lot of friends over here. One day we visited local heavy metal concert of the year 2000. This action was happened in great citadel. Music group was successful. A dancing girl with fire on the stage was something, what is for this Islamic country almost impossible. It was a group of emigrants from Syria. Now they are living in Canada and New Zealand. Some local people try to get to citadel by claiming the tall wall. Some were successful. Others were caught by local police, because they had no tickets. After that we used train from Aleppo to Damascus. It cost around 1 USD. We got a room in a guest house. We found out, that some tourists from Czech Republic were here, because we found 2 days old newspapers from Czech Republic. I got friendly with one girl from Iraq, who every year visited Damascus to meet some people from West. What I can say about Aleppo and Damascus. I prefer historical Aleppo. Damascus was full of rush. But maybe it is just my feeling, who knows.

About 28.10.2000

In one or two days we continued into Jordan. Vlad pukes from car. I was ok, maybe because I was drinking whole way bottle of Czech rum every day little bit. We got capital of Kingdom of Jordan, city Amman. Again we checked internet. We found, that we cannot visit Iraq and Saudi Arabia. After some days we visited Dead Sea. We bathed over there and put this healthy sand on ours skin.

About 1.11.2000

We continued to south to check one of the oldest cities of the world, Petra. We visited city Aqaba. We continued in trip to city Petra next day. Vlad celebrated his birthday on local disco. I left alone city Petra. I used hitchhiking to Tel-Aviv. After 12 hours I got Tel-Aviv. I already played one of local desk game with Ilanit, my old Jewish friend, in her apartment. What I can say about travelling by hitchhiking from Europe to Israel, it could take around 10 days without over stops and other places of interest. After some days I met Vlad again. We were in contact by email. He called me to my mobile phone. He visited Egypt and he met without any dating Jana. She is one of girl which was with us last weekend on trip in east-south part of Czech Republic, before we left Czech Republic on this trip around the world. It is very funny to meet somebody in Africa without dating. Vlad left to Sri Lanca, because he got angry to Jews, because they took land of Palestine. They were killing so many children without reason and maybe just for fun, how he said. I visited with other Jewish girl for tree day's spiritual festival. About 30000 people were there, it was cool.

28. 4. 2001

I was leaving Tel-Aviv. For last 5 months I was living in Tel-Aviv. It is impossible to feel boring here. After I visited Jerusalem again. Before half year I was in Jerusalem, on the way from Jordan to Tel-Aviv, but because I wanted catch annual celebration of Love in Tel-Aviv, then I passed only this city in that time. For troubles in this area, I could not visit Palestine in this time. I was leaving again civilization. I moved into Kingdom of Jordan.

2. 5. 2001

I fly from Amman over Qatar to Sri Lanca. I got cultural and religion shock. In only one week I have been in Jewish, Muslim and Buddhist culture.

3.5.2001

I met with Japanese boy, in capital of Sri Lanca, in Colombo. His name is Rei. He is still very close friend of my. We visited together fantastic city Kandy in mountains in middle of Sri Lanca. We split after that. After few days we met again in other town. Sri Lanca is very small country. I met many tourists again and again on this fantastic island. I visited town Badulla and I visited some falls with one Buddhist family. It was great, because I can check how this family feel about Buddhist religion. In Colombo I met some guy, who call himself pilot of some airline. We drank some beers together and he told me, if I will give him some money for post stamp, then he will send some original tea of Ceylon to my family. I do not want to this, so he left me without say bye to me, because he get drank. After I found, then my friend Vlad met him also in Sri Lanca. Really, it is very small place, where is very easy to find others again and again. I visited south coast of Sri Lanca. There are very beautiful beaches. I have to recommend it to everyone. In this moment I have been in most south geographical width of my life, very close to south hemisphere. In this moment I turn all my spiritual energy to north to be ready to across mysterious India and be able to continue so far to top of the world.

19.5.2001

I took plane from city Colombo, Sri Lanca, to city Trivandrum, India. City Trivandrum is on the most south part of magic India. On bus station near airport I left my pocket with all my credit cards, money and passport, but somebody let me know about it. It was nice to know, that there are nice and kind people around me. In first moment I feel, I lost myself. I have not idea, where I have to go. What I can eat here. India is very large for me in this moment, full of the people. India is very rushed, not clean and not peaceful like Sri Lanca, where you can found streets without people. I found that it is hard to by alone in India in this moment. People speak to me, ask me for money. They watched on me all the time, because I was so different from them. They does not care, if they touch my body or asked me to give them food or drink, what I was just eating or drinking. I was very interesting for them. In United States of America it is totally opposite. People are mostly in own home. Or they are sitting in the car and they are driving to somewhere. Some Americans do not speak with you easy like people in Asia. Americans for sure say: excuse me or sorry, even if they just come close to you. America is for me very clean and very formal, it is very abnormal. But India is for me for sure very unclean, totally without basic rights, but for sure also very abnormal. India is not safety for poor reasons, but safety for religions and culture. America is not safety for rich reasons, but safety for police and very high rights of everyone. But I like to visit America and India again and again, because it is very interesting. It is like travelling in the time.

So I was in India now, I was going to find my friend Vlad to Kodaikanal. I had to take train to city Madurai. Local people were very nice to me in train, so I could peacefully sleep. I did not feel any dangerous to me at first night in train in southern point of this subcontinent. I visited next day biggest original Hindu temple of south India in city Madurai.
